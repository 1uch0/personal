About Me

I’m an Electronics Engineer with an MSc in Mechatronics Engineering and a PhD in Electrical Engineering specializing in Industrial and Smart Systems. My career blends hands-on engineering, academic research, and innovation across multiple countries and industries.

Passion: I thrive at the intersection of technology, research, and real-world applications, with a focus on industrial automation, bio-inspired systems, human–machine interfaces, and AI-driven manufacturing. I enjoy turning innovative concepts into practical solutions that connect technology and people.

🚀 Career Journey

🇪🇸🇷🇺🇩🇪 Europe & Russia — Earned my MSc in Mechatronics Engineering at University of Oviedo, with research stays at Ivanovo State Power University (Russia) and Karlsruhe University of Applied Sciences (Germany).

🇩🇪 Germany — Joined FESTO in the Future Concepts & Modular Automation department, developing a concept for information exchange in process model development.

🇵🇹 Portugal — Research Engineer at UNINOVA, while pursuing a PhD at Nova University of Lisbon.
